Savas Labs Email Signature Generator

  1. Fill out and submit the form below, which will download your signature file
  2. Open your signature_firstname_lastname.html file in Google Chrome
  3. Click the body of the webpage, select all (⌘ + a), and copy (⌘ + c) the page content
  4. Go to Google Mail Settings and paste (⌘ + v) the email signature into the the Signature field
  5. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Changes
